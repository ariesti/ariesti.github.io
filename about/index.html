About Fingertip

Essentially a digital, self-published zine made for the everyday creator taking the leap from consumer to content creator.

Fingertip is an up and coming online zine developed for the everyday creatorâ€”those transitioning from consumer to creator. We're eager to discuss novel topics related to content creation, design, and understanding data. Our readers are in for a treat!

When it was founded in 2018, Fingertip started out as a static gh-pages repo. Following more recent development in static blogging, we've decided to stay a GitHub project, because we can. Things are a lot faster now than they were in the past, with ample room to host images, files, and downloads.

Why Here, Why Now?

This past decade has witnessed a rapid change in our digital landscape. More and more great and relatable content come from plain folks like us. We're here to take a deep dive into the different facets of the creator economy. Whether you're a hardcore influencer making niche videos, or a casual blogger who's just interested in talking about take-out onion rings once or twice a month, we've all got something to share. What better time to do it then here and now?

Our History

Fingertip was founded in 2018 by Lovelli Fuad, who envisioned Fingertip as an opinionated and niche resource for online zine publishing (where people express themselves through art and writing). Having a background in the humanities, she is interested in how our complex online identities and varying opinions take online forms. She started all this with a do-it-yourself attitude and a craving for creativity.
